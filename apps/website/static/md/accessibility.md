# ACCESSIBILITY STATEMENT

Aionsoft is committed to ensuring our website and customer portal are accessible to everyone, including people with disabilities. We aim to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.2 Level AA.

## Ongoing Efforts

* We conduct periodic accessibility reviews and remediate issues we identify or that are reported to us.
* New content and features are designed with accessibility in mind and are tested during development.

## Feedback & Supports

If you experience any difficulty using our site or notice content you believe is not fully accessible, please contact us:

* Email: **hello@aionsoft.io** (subject: “Accessibility”)

Please include the page URL, a description of the issue, your browser and assistive technology (if applicable), and any steps to reproduce. We take your feedback seriously and strive to respond within 5 business days.

## Third-Party Content

Some pages may include integrations or content from third parties. While we do not control these vendors, we encourage them to provide content that is accessible. If you encounter an issue with third-party content on our site, please let us know so we can work on reasonable alternatives.
