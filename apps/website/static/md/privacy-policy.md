# PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: September 1, 2025

Aionsoft LLC (“Aionsoft,” “we,” “our,” or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our website, customer portal, and services.

## 1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

* **Account & Contact Information** – Name, company name, email address, phone number, billing contact, and other information you provide when becoming a customer or contacting us.
* **Account & Login Data** – Login credentials, authentication data, session cookies, access logs, and related information needed to operate customer portals or protected areas.
* **Billing & Payment Information** – Billing details, service history, invoices, subscription information, and payment-related records. Payment card information is processed by Stripe and is not stored directly by us.
* **Customer Portal & Project Data** – Information, files, messages, business data, documents, form submissions, support requests, workflow data, or other materials you provide through our customer portal or as part of our services.
* **Technical & Usage Data** – IP address, browser type, device type, operating system, pages visited, referring pages, timestamps, general location, and diagnostic logs.
* **Cookies & Login Data** – We use cookies primarily in the customer portal to maintain login sessions and secure access. We do not use tracking cookies on our main marketing site.
* **Analytics Data** – We collect limited, non-identifying usage data to understand how visitors use our website and improve our services.
* **Payment Processing** – We use Stripe for billing and subscription payments. Stripe may securely store customer information for recurring billing. Stripe’s privacy policy governs their use of that information.

### Analytics

We may use privacy-friendly analytics tools, such as Swetrix or similar services, to understand general website usage. Analytics data may include page views, device type, browser type, referral source, approximate location, and similar usage information.

We do not use analytics data to identify individual visitors, and we do not use tracking cookies on our main marketing site.

## 2. How We Use Information

We use information to:

* Provide and maintain our services.
* Process billing and manage customer accounts.
* Improve our website and services.
* Communicate with you about support, updates, or service notices.

**We do not sell personal information to third parties.**

## 3. Sharing of Information

We share information only as reasonably necessary to provide our services:

* **Payment Providers** (Stripe) for billing.
* **Hosting & Infrastructure Providers** to operate our systems.

We do not share customer data for advertising or unrelated purposes.

## 4. Data Retention

We retain customer account and billing records as long as necessary to manage services and comply with legal obligations. If you terminate services, we may retain billing records as required by law.

## 5. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have specific rights regarding your personal information. To exercise any of these rights, please email us at **hello@aionsoft.io**.

### For California Residents (CPRA)

If you are a California resident, you have the following rights under the California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA):

* **Right to Know:** You can request access to the specific pieces of personal information we have collected about you.
* **Right to Deletion:** You can ask us to delete your personal information, though this may be subject to legal and business requirements.
* **Right to Opt-Out of Sale/Sharing:** You can request to opt-out of the sale or sharing of your personal information. Please note that we do not sell or share personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising.
* **Right to Correction:** You can request that we correct any inaccurate personal information we hold about you.

## 6. Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. These measures include, but are not limited to, data encryption, access controls, and regular security audits. While we strive to protect your data, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is completely secure. We cannot guarantee its absolute security.

## 7. Children’s Privacy

Our services are not directed to anyone under 18 years old. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 18 without parental consent, we will take steps to remove that information from our systems.
